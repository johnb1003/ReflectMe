What is a Smart Mirror?

Smart mirrors are an up-and-coming trend in the tech industry. This trend transforms your every-day mirror into a futuristic dashboard for all of your personal data needs. They leverage a hidden light display behind a two way mirror surface to give the illusion that the mirror itself is displaying the visuals for whatever information you want to be shown. Since they are often created to display data specific to the user, they are very commonly done in a do-it-yourself fashion, though there are now some commercial out-of-the-box options available.

DIY Smart Mirror Considerations

If you are looking to build your own Smart Mirror, there are several aspects to consider. The main ones being the price you are willing to pay and the size and quality that you are going for. All of these considerations should directly influence which materials you decide to purchase for your mirror. The four main components you will need to buy and assemble are a reflective surface, a light display, a micro computer or processor, and a frame to tie it all together. This can seem like an overwhelming task due to the wide array of options available for each component, but do not stress! There is an awesome and supportive community of DIY Smart Mirror builders eager to help you along the way. Out of the endless DIY Smart Mirror resources found on the web, the one we recommend most is https://smartmirrorguide.com.
