Smart Mirror Components

DIY smart mirrors come in many different shapes and sizes, and at a wide range of costs. Since our goal is to deliver a product that is customized specific to each individual user, we will explain the overall process that goes into creating a smart mirror, rather than giving a detailed tutorial of how to make one specific type.

Broadly speaking, smart mirrors consist of 4 main components: a reflective surface, a light display, a micro computer, and a frame. Below we will describe the role that each component plays and some things to consider when choosing which materials to purchase for the job.
